Kilometers and Photographs

My name is LJ Candelario and this is my travel blog.
I am a chef and a runner at heart. In 2019, fate led me here to New Zealand and I've been blessed to live in this beautiful country ever since. Kilometers and Photographs was created as a project to apply the skills I learned in web development along with my journey of discovering all that New Zealand has to offer.
Come with me as I document this amazing journey!
